ABOUT

Embrace the rebellious spirit of sonic chaos with "The Hackers", where alt rock vibes collide with electrifying riffs and contagious energy. Unleashing a sonic revolution, join us as we hack into your heart and ignite your musical rebellion!

If you're like us and enjoy penetrating an unsuspecting website as much as you like having your ear holes penetrated by some dope melodies; thenour musical style is just right for you.

We come from humble roots. The band members met while performing various nefarious tasks while working with the infamous hacker group "Anonymous". After a few close calls with the law the members decided that musical performance was a much better long term profession.
